I feel like I've done everything. Pizza maker. Shoe shiner. Property manager. Used car salesman. Even a touring musician. But it wasn't until I rediscovered my love for coding that I truly felt like I was in my environment.

(Pun intended.)

Although my professional engineering career may have begun in 2022, I began coding when I was still in the single digits, devouring an HTML for Dummies book and building my own sites. (Back when CSS was still in its infancy.) Now I spend my time working with Angular, Typescript, React, and C#, whether that means designing a game for my son or working to improve my company's product.

Ultimately, my passion for software development combines my love for creating (especially considering my musical background) with my enjoyment of problem-solving and that rush you get when you finally figure it out. (Until you realize you didn't and have to try again, but that's part of the journey, right?) I am always open to learning new languages and technologies, and to say I'm excited about where my future is taking me is an understatement.
